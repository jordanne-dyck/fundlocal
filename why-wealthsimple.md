# Why Wealthsimple?

You've built a company that challenges traditional systems and reimagines what's possible. Now you're standing up a team to do it again with AI, not by layering it onto existing workflows but by rethinking how the work gets done. That's a rare and bold bet, and I think it's the right one.

What drove me was an intuition for what's coming next and a passion to build toward it. At DECIEM, what I built enabled the company to scale to $150M+ in annual DTC revenue at ~30% YoY growth. But these numbers aren't what I'm most proud of. It's that the entrepreneurial culture gave me the flexibility to build cross-functionally owning the digital experience end-to-end with empathy and purpose. The through-line isn't a function — it's a pattern of forethought and building, across whatever disciplines that requires.

That's why Wealthsimple. You're not hiring for a lane — you're hiring for the pattern. And this is where I want to apply it next: a company that's already proven it can rethink an industry, building a team to do it again with the most consequential technology shift of our careers - possibly ever. That's rare, and it matters to me. I see where AI is headed for financial services, and the organizations that move now — with that same combination of ambition and care — will define what the industry becomes.

Here I am.
